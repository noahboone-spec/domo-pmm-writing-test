Domo Apps Positioning

Concise positioning statements describing the value of Domo Apps for each target persona.

IT Leader
The Technical Persona

Domo Apps consolidates custom application development, governance, and distribution into a single governed platform—eliminating the tool sprawl and shadow IT that fragment most data ecosystems. With low-code, pro-code, and AI-powered development paths (App Studio, App Dev Framework, and App Catalyst), IT can empower every business unit to build role-specific applications without sacrificing architectural control. Every app—whether built by a citizen developer or generated via natural language—automatically inherits existing row-level permissions, column-level masking, and per-user data access policies. The result: business teams move faster while IT maintains a single, composable control plane for data, logic, and security across the enterprise.

Business Analyst / Citizen Developer
The Non-Technical Persona

Domo Apps bridges the gap between data insights and business execution by putting app-building power directly in the hands of the people closest to the work. With App Studio's intuitive low-code tools or App Catalyst's natural-language interface, business analysts can go from idea to interactive, data-powered application in hours—no developer queue, no stale spreadsheet exports, no waiting on IT. Apps are built on live, governed data from over 1,000 connected sources, so the information is always current and the governance is already built in. It’s the fastest path from “I wish we had a tool for this” to a production-ready app your entire team can use.
