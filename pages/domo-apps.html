Speed for your teams.
Control for IT.

Every app — whether built by a citizen developer, generated by AI, or coded by an engineer, automatically inherits your organization's existing data governance. No extra configuration. No security gaps.

Citizen development doesn't mean ungoverned chaos. Apps consume governed data, standardized transformations, and shared metrics from the moment they're created.
