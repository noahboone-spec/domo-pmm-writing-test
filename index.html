How It Was Built

Research-first, KG-grounded process

Every deliverable traces back to canonical sources. No claims were invented. External data supplements but never overrides the Knowledge Graph.

Step 1

Knowledge Graph Queries

Pulled canonical messaging from 10+ KG queries: Domo Apps (Layer 4d), App Catalyst (Layer 4a-7), persona frameworks, governance docs, and all 7 product profiles.

~45 min · KG API via Python
Step 2

Competitive & Market Research

13 web searches mapping 7 competitors (Power Apps, Salesforce, ServiceNow, Appian, Mendix, OutSystems, AppSheet). Market stats from Gartner, Forrester, Retool.

~30 min · Web research
Step 3

Figma & Brand Inspection

Cataloged 97+ components, 69 colors, and full typography scale from the Domo Figma design system. Cross-referenced with domo-design-playbook.md.

~15 min · Figma MCP
Step 4

Writing & Code

Wrote all positioning, hero sections, champion slide, and landing page. Every claim traced to KG canonical. KG audit caught and fixed 4 derivative phrasings.

~60 min · Claude Code
